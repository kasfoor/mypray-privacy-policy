Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

MyPray ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the MyPray app ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Location Data. The App requests access to your approximate location solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Prayer Data. All prayer tracking data (which prayers you have completed, streaks, etc.) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data.

Advertising Identifiers. The App uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and usage data to serve relevant ads. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • Location is used exclusively to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • Prayer data is used only to display your personal tracking history within the App.
  • Advertising identifiers are used by AdMob to display third-party advertisements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described below:

  • Google AdMob — receives device advertising identifiers for ad serving. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your location or prayer data with any third party.

4. Data Retention

All prayer tracking data remains on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App. We do not retain any personal data on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Choices

  • Location: You can deny or revoke location access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → MyPray.
  • Ad Tracking: You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Notifications: You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings → Notifications → MyPray.

7. Security

We rely on Apple's platform security for all on-device data storage. No prayer or personal data is transmitted from your device by us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
